OUR CREATIVE CURRICULUM

We focus on the process of stimulating Science in a class environment.
involve the stakeholders of a successful lesson; the learner and the teacher.

Electronics

In an ordinary African classroom lesson, this branch of Physics looks so abstract and no wonder some curriculum developers have suggested it should not be introduced until someone has specialised in Physics at an advanced level. However, we prove that electronics concepts can easily be mastered by first stimulating a learner through practical electronics applied in building a robot.

Mechanics

This is one of the science concepts applied in a robot build up. It is a branch of Physics which learners find difficulty understanding from plain classroom lessons. At Fundi Bots, the journey of a robot equips a learner with practical mechanics even before they know it!

Computer Programming

In order to give a sense of behaviour to a robot, one needs the knowledge of computer programming. This, at the end of the process, produces learners who are not only computer literate, but are also able to use computer knowledge to create solutions for the problems in their communities.

OUR PROGRAMS

In order to reach out to as many young people as possible, we have divided our science campaign in to a number of programs:
Schools outreach, Fundi Girls, Training of Trainers, Fundi Space Access.

SCHOOLS OUTREACH

Schools Outreach remains at the centre of our main objective in all our centers. This is a time when our Fundis visit the partnership schools to conduct trainings. Some of the schools decide to integrate Fundi Bots curriculum into their school programme so we visit such schools on a weekly basis.
